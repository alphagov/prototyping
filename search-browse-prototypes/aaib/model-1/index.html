What we do

We investigate civil aircraft accidents and serious incidents within the UK, its overseas territories and provide assistance and expertise to the international air accident community to improve aviation safety globally.

Our purpose is to improve aviation safety by determining the causes of air accidents and serious incidents and making safety recommendations intended to prevent recurrence. It is not to apportion blame or liability.
