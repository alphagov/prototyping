Introduction

The Affordable Housing Capital Funding Guide (AHCFG) sets out the policy and procedural requirements and guidance for organisations allocated grant funding under the HCA's 2011-15 Affordable Homes Programme on or after 01 April 2011. This version of the AHCFG supersedes all previous editions. See archived versions of this document

Contents

  1. General

    The Affordable Housing Capital Funding Guide (AHCFG) sets out the policy and procedural requirements and guidance for organisations allocated grant funding under the HCA's 2011-15 Affordable Homes Programme on or after 01 April 2011.

  2. Housing for sent

    Affordable rent, social rent and repair

  3. Housing for sale

    Within the 2011-15 Affordable Housing Programme, there are two purchase products which fall under the HomeBuy/FIRST STEPS banner.

  4. Mortgage rescue

    This chapter sets out the main features and procedures that must be followed and the conditions under which RPs must process the mortgage rescue Mortgage Rescue Scheme.

  5. Specialist programmes

    Homelessness Change Programme, Care and Support Specialised Housing Fund, Traveller Pitch Funding and Empty Homes 2012-15

  6. Grant recovery

    Following the Housing and Regeneration Act 2008, the responsibility for the administration and policy making for the Disposal Proceeds Fund rests with Regulator.
