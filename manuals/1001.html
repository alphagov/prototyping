  1. 1001.1 – The criteria

    What is agile, why it works and how to do it

    Agile methodologies will help you and your team to build world-class, user-centred services quickly and affordably. This way of working can be very different for those used to long specifications and procurement processes, but it’s essential.

    Agile projects

    An agile project is about creating fast iterations of products based on the feedback of real users.

    It means regularly releasing small pieces of functionality; constant communication between team members, and using equipment that displays progress being made by your team.

    User stories

    User understanding is the most important factor when creating a service, so you need to have a clear understanding of what users. This way of working can be very different for those used to long specifications.

    It means regularly releasing small pieces of functionality; constant communication between team members.
