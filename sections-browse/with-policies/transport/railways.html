What is the government doing about railways?

The government is expanding and improving the rail network to improve passeners' journeys, support economic growth and to provide the rail capacity that Britain needs through major infrastructure projects. We plan and invest in transport infrastructure to keep the UK on the move.

Policies and major programmes

  • Expanding and improving the rail network
  • Making transport more accessible to all
  • High Speed Two (HS2)
