VAT Notice 701/19 Fuel and power

Organisation:

HM Revenue & Customs

Page history:
Published 18 February 2014

SUMMARY: This Notice explains how suppliers and users should treat supplies of fuel and power for VAT purposes. It also tells you how to treat a number of other supplies connected with fuel and power.
