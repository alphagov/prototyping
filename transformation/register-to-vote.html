Benefits for users

If you’re eligible to vote in the UK you will be able to apply to register quickly and easily online.

Business case

ERTP will change voter registration by making it individual (no longer done by the ‘head of the household’), creating a new digital channel and confirming identities before accepting registrations. There will be a large set-up cost: ERTP is being done for policy reasons (to ensure elections are fair), not to save money. If ERTP produces a higher quality voter register, it may ultimately help abolish the annual “canvass” (which confirms the voter list every year): this would be a large saving, but no decision has been taken.
