Overview

Import

Importing from outside the EU is subject to a third country duty of 4.70%. Goods are also subject to VAT standard rate of 20%.

Imports are subject to Import control on cat and dog fur.

Export

There are no EU third country measures for this commodity. There are no VAT or Exise measures for this commodity.

See import and export tables for country-specific measures and restrictions.

This tariff applies today (21/5/2012)
