Phase 1

Initial findings and decision

The OFT has decided to refer the completed acquisition by Ryanair Holdings for further investigation, due to competition concerns. The OFT has concluded that Ryanair's stake may give it the ability to exercise material influence over the commercial policy of Aer Lingus and that there is a realistic prospect that its stake has resulted or will result in a substantial lessening of competition on a number of Ryanair and/or Aer Lingus routes between the UK and Ireland. The OFT therefore considers it appropriate to have a more detailed investigation into this matter.

Invitation to comment - now closed

Completed acquisition by Ryanair Holdings plc of a minority interest in Aer Lingus Group plc
Reference number: ME/4694/10

The Office of Fair Trading is considering whether arrangements are in progress or in contemplation which, if carried into effect, will result in the creation of a relevant merger situation under the merger provisions of the Enterprise Act 2002 and, if so, whether the creation of that situation may be expected to result in a substantial lessening of competition within any market or markets in the United Kingdom.

Affected sector: Airlines
