Niche Mainstream

Product manager: Ben Andrews

User needs

As a mainstream user who needs the 20% information that is detailed, included in a long list of variants or is potentially specialist, I need to access this information without diluting the simplicity of 80% mainstream content

Insights

Layered "onion" model of content need

The onion model describes a layered approach to content, in which 80% of users find what they need from very simple, very readable overviews, undiluted by all the possible variations, whilst detail, variations and more technical topics are handled by Niche mainstream content.

This content can be less readable (i.e. anchor links with lists of variations) because people will only read the sections relevant to them.

Specifically technical content can be handled by Specialist formats and finally by Legislation.
